The darkest top-down 2D role-playing game released.

Made for Windows

Made for Linux

Welcome Tony. For you, tomorrow is always darker than yesterday. But you must carry on.

By maintaining your mental health, you must make your way through seven stages of life. No day is the same and every action has a reaction.

Tony is created in C# with the monogame library. Currently, Tony is compatible with Windows and Linux - however other platforms will come soon.
